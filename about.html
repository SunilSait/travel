Our Story

Born From a Love of India

WanderIndia was founded in 2012 by a group of passionate travelers who believed that India's incredible diversity deserved to be explored, not just visited. What started as a small desk in a Bangalore apartment has grown into one of India's most trusted domestic travel agencies.

Today, we're a team of 80+ travel enthusiasts, local experts, and logistics wizards who collectively have explored every corner of this beautiful country. Our mission is simple: help every traveler discover their own India story.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make exploring India accessible, affordable, and unforgettable for every traveler — whether you seek adventure, culture, relaxation, or spiritual enrichment. We handle the details so you can focus on the experience.

Our Vision

To be India's most loved domestic travel brand — where every journey creates lifelong memories, supports local communities, and inspires travelers to explore more of the world's greatest country.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

From a small startup to India's trusted travel partner — here's how we grew.

2012
Founded in Bangalore

Three travel enthusiasts start WanderIndia from a small office with just 5 Kerala packages.

2014
Expanded to 50 Destinations

Rapid growth across South and North India. Team expands to 20 travel experts.

2016
First Major Award

Recognized as "Best Emerging Domestic Travel Agency" by India Tourism Awards.

2018
10,000 Travelers Milestone

Celebrated our 10,000th happy traveler with a special traveler Ladakh expedition.

2020
Digital Transformation

Launched online booking platform and virtual trip consultation services during COVID.

2024
50,000 Travelers & Counting

Now serving 50,000+ travelers annually with 200+ destinations and 500+ packages.
