Best Time To Visit

October to March. Winters are beautiful in Rajasthan with cool nights and warm, sunny days. Ideal for camel safaris, desert camping, and exploring massive forts without the heat.

Top Places To See

Visit the magnificent Hawa Mahal and Amer Fort in Jaipur, the romantic Lake Palace in Udaipur, the golden sand dunes of Jaisalmer, and the towering Mehrangarh Fort in Jodhpur.

Pro Travel Tips

Hire government-approved guides at forts to hear the fascinating royal histories. Jaisalmer desert camps are a must-try for folk dances. Carry for temperature drops at night.

Best Time To Visit

November to February is prime beach weather. The climate is breezy, water sports are fully operational, and shacks are vibrant. October and March are great for quieter vibes.

Top Places To See

Relax at the peaceful Agonda and Palolem beaches in South Goa, explore the active cliffs of Vagator in North Goa, walk through historic Fontainhas, and witness the roaring Falls.

Pro Travel Tips

South Goa is perfect for luxury wellness and peaceful beaches. North Goa is the capital of music festivals and night markets. Rent a scooter for cheap, scenic coastal driving.

Best Time To Visit

March to June for pleasant valleys, trekking, and escaping the summer heat. December to February for fresh snow, skiing in Solang, and winter wonderland sights.

Top Places To See

Explore the Mall Road and Jakhoo Temple in Shimla, experience adventure at Solang Valley and Rohtang Pass in Manali, visit monasteries and witness the cold deserts of Spiti.

Pro Travel Tips

Pack dynamic layers; mountain weather can fluctuate fast. Apply for Rohtang passes weeks in advance as entries are restricted. Drink mineral water to ease altitude acclimatization.

Best Time To Visit

October to March is the absolute best time. The temperatures are mild, making it very comfortable to walk along ghats, witness evening prayer rituals, and raft on the Ganges.

Top Places To See

Witness the sacred Dashashwamedh Ghat in Varanasi, visit the iconic Lakshman Jhula and Beatles Ashram in Rishikesh, attend the Ganga Aarti in Haridwar.

Pro Travel Tips

Dress conservatively near religious sites. Book early morning boat rides in Varanasi to catch the serene sunrise rituals. Rishikesh is perfect for combining yoga with rafting.
